Joseph advises companies and senior professionals on all aspects of employment law, both in contentious and non-contentious matters. He has a particular expertise in the insurance market, advising clients on team moves and restraint of trade, business re-organisations, senior hires and exits, and boardroom disputes.

He also advises in the banking, financial, shipping and recruitment sectors, for clients based both in the UK and internationally.

{% assign titles = titles | append: "Career Overview" | append: "|" %} {% capture accordion %}

Joseph was originally called to the Bar in 2000 before being admitted as a Solicitor in 2003. He worked in both private practice, including at Holman Fenwick Willan, and in-house at Dresdner Kleinwort Investment Bank before founding his own employment law practice, Joseph Sutton Solicitors, in 2010. In 2016 he also became a Consultant at Carter Perry Bailey.

Joseph speaks Portuguese (Brazilian) and Greek.

Experience Highlights
  • Represented a niche shipping agency in High Court proceedings against a former employee.
  • Represented the Global Head of Compliance at a large banking group.
