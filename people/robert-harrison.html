Robert conducts national and international insurance and reinsurance disputes on behalf of a variety of clients (underwriters, brokers and agents) predominantly regarding coverage issues across a broad spectrum of policy types. His practice encompasses both litigation and arbitration matters.

In addition, he advises on regulatory and compliance issues and run-off strategies.

More recently, he has been engaged in the establishment of captive and cell captive formations, primarily in Malta. He qualified as a Solicitor of the Republic of Ireland in 2018.

Robert has acted for insurers in professional indemnity claims, including accountants, auditors, surveyors, architects and solicitors.

Robert speaks Dutch, French and Spanish.

{% accordion 'Career Overview' %}

He is experienced in major complex loss casualty claims, including oil and chemical losses and rail disasters. He has, as a result, been retained to advise upon disaster management planning for both the Heathrow Express and Heathrow Terminal 5 projects at the instruction of BAA.

Throughout his career, Robert has been particularly involved in all-manner of commercial disputes involving both his insurer and reinsurer clients.

Having qualified in 1984, Robert has acted for those within the insurance and reinsurance industry during the course of his career spanning over 30 years.

Robert joined Carter Perry Bailey as a Consultant in 2012, having been a Partner and Consultant at several leading insurance law firms, in particular Vizards Wyeth.
